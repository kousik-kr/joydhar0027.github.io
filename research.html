Core Direction

Efficient, multimodal, and trustworthy AI for heterogeneous healthcare data.

My research prioritizes reliability, transferability, and computational realism across medical imaging, multi-omics, EHR, and time-series signals.

ML Foundations

Multimodal AI

Multimodal representation learning and fusion with multimodal and cross-modal attention for heterogeneous medical data.

Trustworthy AI

Adversarial attacks, certified and empirical adversarial robustness, and curriculum learning for dependable healthcare models.

Generative AI

Generative adversarial networks, diffusion models, autoencoders, and variational autoencoders for medical AI workflows.

Healthcare AI

Efficient medical image analysis, heterogeneous clinical data modeling, disease classification, and segmentation.
