Profile

Building trustworthy AI where clinical reality is noisy, multimodal, and high-stakes.

I am a Ph.D. Scholar and Deep Learning Researcher at IIT Ropar working at the intersection of Efficient AI, Multimodal AI, and Trustworthy AI for healthcare. My work spans heterogeneous medical data, including medical imaging, multi-omics, electronic health records, and time-series signals.
